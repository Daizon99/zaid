Best Formation Campus Ottawa honors a 30-year tradition of excellence in college education. The only private bilingual college in the Outaouais region, Best Formation is an authorized institution subsidized by the Quebec Ministry of Higher Education. It offers quality teaching, personalized supervision adapted to each student, a modern and welcoming learning environment and a wide range of DEC (Diploma of Collegial Studies), pre-university and technical programs, as well as AEC (Attestation of Collegial Studies).
